Welcome to CareFolio

An Online Revalidation Portfolio

CareFolio is an online electronic portfolio hosting website.
In 2016 the Nursing and Midwifery Council will introduce revalidation, a requirement that all members must keep and record their own continued professional development in the form of a portfolio.
CareFolio is here to help with this daunting task. With a CareFolio account you can manage your entire professional development and stay up to date in a manner that is convenient to you.
