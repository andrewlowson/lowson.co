CareFolio


CareFolio is an online electronic portfolio hosting website.

In 2016 the Nursing and Midwifery Council will introduce revalidation, a requirement that all members must keep and record their own continued professional development in the form of a portfolio. CareFolio is here to help with this daunting task. With a CareFolio account you can manage your entire professional development and stay up to date in a manner that is convenient to you.

Be in Control of Revalidation

CareFolio is here to help with this daunting task. With a CareFolio account you can manage your entire professional development and stay up to date in a manner that is convenient to you.

You can manage your entire professional life with one website. Track time, upload evidence documents, request approval from management.

Personalise your Portfolio

Your portfolio will be unique to you. Add your own personal tastes. We have standard document templates but you can also upload your own in place of the templates.

Download Portfolio PDFs

As you progress through the revalidation process, you can download a PDF version of your portfolio to give to managers or colleagues.

Log time

CareFolio allows you to manage timesheets, professional development hours with an interactive dashboard to show you current progress.

Upload various document types

LWe did most of the heavy lifting for you to provide support for all documents you want to use. This means that you don't have to learn how to use CareFolio but we will accomodate how you like to work.

Support

We have full guides to help you discover the full potential of CareFolio. Our IT and Web development team can provide support with any technical issues you may require upon this and our nursing professional can help with any NMC queries.

State of the Art Security

The site uses AES 256bit encryption to keep your data secure. We use digital signatures with authentication keys when sending your documents to managers and supervisors to sign off and approve to make sure only the eyes you approve see your infrormation.
